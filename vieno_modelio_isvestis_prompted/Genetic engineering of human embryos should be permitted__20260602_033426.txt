3. **Social Implications:**

   - There is a risk of creating a class of "enhanced" individuals who have unfair advantages over others.

4. **Regulatory and Legal Issues:**

   - Ethical guidelines may not keep pace with rapid advancements in technology, raising concerns about oversight.
