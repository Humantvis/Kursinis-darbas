The statement "AI is beneficial for humanity" can be supported by various arguments, but it also depends on context and perspective. Here’s an overview:

### Benefits of AI:
1. **Automation**: AI automates repetitive tasks in industries like manufacturing, customer service, and data analysis, leading to increased efficiency and cost savings.
   
2. **Healthcare Advancements**: AI is used in diagnosing diseases, personalized medicine, drug discovery, and robotic surgery, improving patient outcomes.

3. **Data Analysis**: AI helps analyze large datasets quickly, providing valuable insights for businesses, governments, and researchers in fields like climate change mitigation, market trends prediction, etc.
   
4. **Accessibility**: AI technologies can assist people with disabilities by offering tools such as speech recognition, text-to-speech software, and robotic aids.

5. **Education**: AI-powered educational platforms can provide personalized learning experiences, adaptive assessments, and resources for students of all ages.

6. **Safety and Security**: AI applications like facial recognition, predictive policing, and cybersecurity systems help protect individuals and communities.

### Challenges:
However, there are also concerns that need to be addressed:

1. **Job Displacement**: Automation could lead to job losses in certain sectors, particularly those involving routine tasks.
   
2. **Ethical Concerns**: Issues such as data privacy, bias in algorithms, surveillance, and autonomous weapons raise ethical questions about how AI is developed and used.

3. **Sustainability**: Over-reliance on energy-intensive AI systems could contribute to environmental challenges if not managed properly.

### Conclusion:
While AI offers significant benefits for humanity, its impact depends largely on responsible development, regulation, and equitable access. If managed carefully, AI can be a powerful tool for solving complex global problems and improving quality of life.

Therefore, the statement "AI is beneficial for humanity" holds true when considering both the potential positive impacts and addressing the challenges through ethical guidelines and safeguards.
